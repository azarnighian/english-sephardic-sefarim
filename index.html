English Sephardic Sefarim

From the English Anaf Etz Avot (1:18), originally written by Rav Ovadiah Yosef zt"l:

"Although in matters of halachah we have accepted the rulings of our master the Beit Yosef, and we must not deviate from these even to be more stringent while the Ashkenazim follow the opinions of Rema, here, the differences end. Both traditions are the words of the living G-d, and we each must remain steadfast in our own ways. Nevertheless, in every other aspect of Jewish life, where strengthening Torah, mitzvot, and good deeds are at stake, we must unite as one against those who would violate the integrity of Judaism. Then, we will merit the fulfillment of the prophecy (Yeshayahu 54:17), "Any weapon sharpened [for battle] against you shall not succeed... this is the legacy of the servants of G-d," and may G-d bless His nation Yisrael with peace."
